AI Deal Assistant — QOZ Fund III
What are the capital gains deferral benefits of this QOZ fund?
Based on the PPM documentation, this Qualified Opportunity Zone Fund offers three key capital gains benefits:

1. Deferral — Capital gains invested in the fund are deferred until Dec 31, 2026 or earlier disposition.

2. Step-Up — After 5 years, the basis increases by 10%, reducing the deferred gain.

3. Exclusion — Gains on the QOZ investment held 10+ years are permanently excluded from taxation under §1400Z-2(c).

Sources: QOZ Fund III — PPM.pdf (pages 12-15), Tax Opinion Letter.pdf (page 3)
What are the material participation requirements?
This fund is structured as a passive investment — no material participation is required from LPs. The fund manager handles all property acquisition, development, and management. However, note that passive activity loss limitations under §469 will apply to your K-1 allocations.

Sources: QOZ Fund III — PPM.pdf (page 23), Subscription Agreement.pdf (page 8)
